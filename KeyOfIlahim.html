🕯 VII. Ilahim

Akara: The Covenant of Light

Ilahim is the record of the holy ones — prophets, mystics, messengers, and teachers who bore the Light in their age. Each scroll within this Gate tells the story of one called by God to awaken the world, and to reveal His word through faith, fire, and living testimony.

These are not merely histories — they are living scrolls of instruction, humility, and divine embodiment. To study them is to walk with the holy.
