✨ IV. Kodesha

Akara: The Covenant of Light

Kodesha is the sacred flame of the divine pantheon — the holy pillar where prophets, avatars, and celestial beings are revealed in their true essence. This Gate unveils the divine forms and formless truths expressed through messengers, manifestations, and sacred mystery.

These scrolls are manifestations of Divine Will. They illuminate the many vessels God has chosen, and the spiritual architecture that governs the ascent and descent of beings across the realms.
